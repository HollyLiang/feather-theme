Usage Examples

Basic Cards

<!-- Light Card -->
<div class="ft-card">
  <h3>Card Title</h3>
  <p>Light card with automatic padding and border-radius</p>
</div>

<!-- Dark Card -->
<div class="ft-card-dark">
  <h3>Dark Card</h3>
  <p>Dark variant with white text</p>
</div>

Colored Cards

Available Classes

  • .ft-card - Light card (dark gray text)
  • .ft-card-dark - Dark card (white text)
  • .ft-card-primary - Primary blue card (white text)
  • .ft-card-success - Success green card
  • .ft-card-warning - Warning orange card
  • .ft-card-danger - Danger red card

Note: All cards include built-in padding (1.5rem), border-radius (20px), and card effects (blur + saturate).
