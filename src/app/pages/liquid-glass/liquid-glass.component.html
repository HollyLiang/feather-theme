Usage Examples

Basic Glass Cards

<!-- Light Glass Card -->
<div class="ft-liquid-glass">
  <h3>Card Title</h3>
  <p>Light glass card with automatic padding and border-radius</p>
</div>

<!-- Dark Glass Card -->
<div class="ft-liquid-glass-dark">
  <h3>Dark Card</h3>
  <p>Dark variant with white text</p>
</div>

Colored Glass Cards

<!-- Primary Glass -->
<div class="ft-liquid-glass-primary">

Available Classes

  • .ft-liquid-glass - Light glass card (dark gray text)
  • .ft-liquid-glass-dark - Dark glass card (white text)
  • .ft-liquid-glass-primary - Primary blue glass (white text)
  • .ft-liquid-glass-success - Success green glass
  • .ft-liquid-glass-warning - Warning orange glass
  • .ft-liquid-glass-danger - Danger red glass

Note: All cards include built-in padding (1.5rem), border-radius (20px), and glass effects (blur + saturate).
